# EN → ZH Glossary

Terminology table for translating Life in the UK content. Translators (Gemini
batches) MUST use these renderings verbatim wherever the term appears.
Maintained by Fable 5; propose additions via the review queue, do not edit ad hoc.

Convention for names: keep the English name and add the Chinese in parentheses
on first mention within a unit, e.g. "威廉一世（William the Conqueror）".
Well-known place names use their standard Chinese exonym without parentheses.
